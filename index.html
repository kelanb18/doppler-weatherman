About

Hi, I'm Mari

I'm an independent stylist at Talula Beauty in Petoskey, Michigan. I specialize in color — balayage, highlights, all-over color — and love creating looks that feel natural and easy to maintain.

As a booth renter, I own my client relationships entirely. That means every appointment gets my full attention, every time. I take time to understand what you want before we touch a single strand.

Whether it's your first color or you're ready for something completely new, I'm here to make the process easy and the results worth talking about.
