DOPPLER WEATHERMAN
Weather as music  ·  Your zip as score

Real weather data from any ZIP code becomes a living generative soundtrack — tempo, harmony, rhythm, and texture all driven by temperature, wind, humidity, and storm intensity.

1
Enter a ZIP code and hit Load. Weather data streams in and the machine reads the sky.
2
Hit Play and listen. The instrument generates music shaped entirely by that location's conditions right now.
3
Cook up a storm — drag the atmosphere sliders to push temperature, wind, rain, and storm intensity however you like. Hit Apply Manual.
4
Shape the sound with the FX panel: reverb, echo, filter, drive, wah, chorus, fuzz, and more. Every slider is live.
5
Play along using the Live Sheet — it shows the current chord progression and last melody note so you can jam on any instrument.
6
Share your sounding — hit ↗ Share to copy a link that locks in your exact ZIP, atmosphere, and every FX setting. Anyone who opens it hears exactly what you built.
